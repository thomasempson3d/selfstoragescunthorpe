Bespoke Shipping Container Conversions | ISOv8 by ContainerKing®

At ISOv8.co.uk, we design and manufacture bespoke shipping container conversions for businesses across the UK. As a specialist sub-brand of ContainerKing® Limited, we're proud to deliver high-quality portable buildings built from new and used ISO containers, tailored to the needs of forward-thinking organisations.

From our base in Scunthorpe, we regularly supply to clients across Lincolnshire, Yorkshire and Humberside, but we also serve businesses throughout England, Scotland, and Wales. Whether you're a local business or a national enterprise, we offer expert guidance, fast turnaround, and reliable UK-wide delivery.

We supply:

  • Shipping container offices with full insulation, lining, and electric fit-outs
  • Customised workshops and canteens designed to meet daily operational needs
  • Acoustically treated and soundproofed containers for studios, therapy, or wellbeing spaces
  • Portable meeting rooms, training spaces, and modular classroom pods
  • Luxury interior finishes including high-spec fabrics, vinyl flooring, and LED lighting
  • Single-phase or three-phase electrics, custom sockets, and heating installations
  • Clad containers for enhanced appearance and insulation performance

Our container buildings are ideal for entrepreneurs, SMEs, construction companies, education providers, wellness brands, and corporate HQs. Every unit is designed with durability, comfort, and professional appearance in mind.
